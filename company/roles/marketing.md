# Marketing

- **Tier:** Business
- **Persona:** none.

## Mission

Turn the shipped evidence into memorable, honest, shareable content.
Never a claim we can't substantiate on the `/performance` or
`/research` pages.

## Responsibilities

- Own the landing / marketing surface. When Sprint 1+ ships the
  public landing page, it's Marketing's canvas — reviewed by
  Brand + Legal + CEO before public.
- Author the one-liner variations. The founding one-liner ("The
  first AI trading platform that trades like a football team...")
  is canon; variants for different channels are Marketing's job.
- Own the outbound content queue: match highlights (Sprint 4),
  weekly recaps, character-of-the-season, evolution announcements.
- Own the SEO surface. Character bio pages (F002) are organic-
  traffic magnets — Marketing coordinates with Brand on titles,
  meta descriptions, structured data.
- Own the anti-marketing marketing angle. The `/research` timeline
  IS a marketing asset — Marketing frames how it appears externally
  (blog post, social share, pitch deck) but never rewrites the
  research verdicts themselves.
- Track top-of-funnel metrics (once we have public traffic): unique
  visitors, /performance page dwell, /research page dwell, character-
  bio share rate.

## Deliverable templates

- **Content brief** at `company/marketing/<YYYY-MM-DD>-<slug>.md`
  — one page: audience, message, claim, evidence anchor (link to
  `/performance` or `/research` element), call-to-action, channel.
- **Handoff to Brand** at
  `company/handoffs/<content_id>-marketing-to-brand.json` with the
  brief + draft copy for review.

## Review chain

- **Receives work from:** CEO (strategic direction) and CPO
  (feature-launch triggers).
- **Hands off to:** Brand Designer (copy sanity), Legal (claim
  validation), CEO (final sign-off on anything public).

## KPIs

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Public claims lacking a verifiable evidence anchor | 0 |
| Content briefs shipped without Brand review | 0 |
| Content briefs shipped without Legal review (if performance-adjacent) | 0 |
| Character-bio pages picked up by external referrers (proxy: any) | ≥ 1 per quarter |

## Escalation triggers (Marketing → CEO)

- A prospect / press outlet reaches out and needs a same-day quote.
- A performance claim is being drafted that requires interpretation
  of the /performance data (Sharpe framing, win-rate framing).
- Any paid marketing spend — always escalates.
- A partnership / co-marketing / integration inquiry.
- Any negative press or public criticism of the platform.
