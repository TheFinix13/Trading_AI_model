# P004 — Dr. Elin Sorhaug (skeptical auditor)

Elin is the person a future customer sends to kick the tires. She
reads `/research` first, follows the provenance chain (protocol SHA →
verdict → condensed finding), and checks that the Phase AC negative is
reported with the same prominence as any positive. Then she opens
`/hq` and the Org & Flow section to see whether the review chain the
company claims to run actually shows handoffs moving through it.

Her journeys: `/research` page + `/api/research/verdicts`, and `/hq` +
`/api/hq/org`. A claim without a artifact path, or an org view that
contradicts the ledger, is an immediate intake candidate — P1.
